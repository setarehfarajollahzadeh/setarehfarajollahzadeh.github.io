I am an Assistant Professor of Operations Management at the Desautels Faculty of Management, McGill University.

My research focuses on operations management, market design, decision analytics, and socially important operational problems. I use mathematical modeling, optimization, and data-driven methods to study how operational systems can improve efficiency, fairness, and access.

Before joining McGill, I received my Ph.D. in Operations Management from the Rotman School of Management at the University of Toronto.
